Te Pā Ecosystem Rhizome

A rhizomatic map after Deleuze & Guattari — no single root. Any node can connect to any other (connection & heterogeneity); the six motifs and six thinkers are plateaus you may enter from any point. Each node carries a Donella Meadows leverage point and a live health metric read from assets/data/ecosystem-metrics.json. Edit that file to re-render the map and measure change over time.
