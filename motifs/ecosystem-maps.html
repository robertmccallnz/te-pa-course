Te Pā Ecosystem Maps

Two system-design maps of the Te Pā ecosystem. The first is grounded in Ōtepoti; the second scales the same logic across Aotearoa. Iwi and Māori organisation nodes are clickable and link to official sites. These links denote reference and relationship, not formal endorsement or partnership.
