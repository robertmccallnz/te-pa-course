Te Pā Ecosystem Maps

Two system-design maps of the Te Pā ecosystem. The first is grounded in Ōtepoti; the second scales the same logic across Aotearoa. Nodes use Māori-first naming, edges are labelled with their whakapapa of exchange, and the Six-Thinkers lenses sit as a dotted overlay applied across every learning pathway.
