Te Pā Ecosystem Maps

System-design maps for Te Pā Tūwatawata, woven through the Six Thinkers meta-layer. Edge labels name the flows: kōrero (dialogue), raraunga (data / sovereignty), kaihaukai (reciprocal exchange), mahika kai (provisioning), takarangi (spiral growth), ngaru (wave / propagation).
